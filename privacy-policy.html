Privacy Policy for Clip Planner

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

To provide our video scheduling service, we collect the following information via the official TikTok API after you grant authorization:

  • Authentication Tokens: Secure OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens to make authenticated API requests on your behalf. These tokens are stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
  • User Profile Information: Your basic public TikTok profile information (e.g., username, profile picture) to display within our application interface and confirm your identity.
  • Video Content: The video files and associated metadata (titles, descriptions, scheduled times) that you provide for the explicit purpose of scheduling and publication.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your session with TikTok.
  • To upload your video content to TikTok's servers as per your request.
  • To publish your scheduled videos at the time you specify.
  • To display your content schedule and history within our application for your reference.

We will never share, sell, or rent your personal data or video content to third parties. We will not use your data for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Security and Retention

We implement robust security measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are encrypted at rest. Video files are temporarily stored on secure servers and are deleted immediately after a successful upload to TikTok.

4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can revoke our app's access to your TikTok account at any time directly through your TikTok account settings. Upon revocation, or upon a direct deletion request sent to vidqueue@support.com, we will permanently delete all your personal data and authentication tokens from our systems within 72 hours.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vidqueue@support.com.
